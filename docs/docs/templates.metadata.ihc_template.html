Chromogenic IHC (please specify one score/metric)
Property Description Data Type
tumor proportion score Tumor Proportion Score (TPS) is the percentage of viable tumor cells showing marker staining relative to all viable tumor cells. (0-1) assays.components.imaging.ihc_entry#tumor_proportion_score
combined positive score Combined Positive Score (CPS) is the percentage of marker staining cells (tumor cells and cells that are non-tumor) relative to all viable tumor cells. (0-1) assays.components.imaging.ihc_entry#combined_positive_score
inflammatory cells Percentage of inflammatory cells (non-tumor cells) showing marker staining relative to all inflammatory cells. (0-1) assays.components.imaging.ihc_entry#inflammatory_cells
positive inflammatory cell area Area of PD-L1+ Inflammatory Cells over the area of TSI + IT as a percentage. (0-1) assays.components.imaging.ihc_entry#positive_inflammatory_cell_area
intensity A measure of the intensity or brightness of the protein. (0-3) assays.components.imaging.ihc_entry#intensity
% expression A percentage of the relevant cells considered positive. (0-100) assays.components.imaging.ihc_entry#percentage_expression
h score A summation of the percentage of area stained at each intensity level multiplied by the weighted intensity. (0-300) assays.components.imaging.ihc_entry#h_score
comment A text comment regarding this slide. assays.components.imaging.ihc_entry#comment
